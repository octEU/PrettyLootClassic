# PrettyLoot

A clean, lightweight loot notification addon for **Classic WoW**. 
Displays items looted, gold gained or lost, reputation changes, 
and auction house sales as a tidy sliding list on your screen.

## Features

- Fully customisable/personal experience
- Item loot notifications with quality colouring and stack counts
- Gold gain/loss display
- Reputation changes with updating faction standing colour
- Auction house sale notifications
- Blacklist and highlight lists
- Highlighted items play a configurable sound and stay on screen longer

## Usage

Type `/pl` to open the options panel, or use `/pl unlock` to drag 
the display to your preferred position.

## Dependencies

- [Ace3](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/ace3)
- [LibSharedMedia-3.0](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/libsharedmedia-3-0)

## Compatibility

Classic Era · Season of Discovery · Anniversary
